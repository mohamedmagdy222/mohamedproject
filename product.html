Writing this 13-inch MacBook Pro 2020 review brings mixed emotions. I’m excited to type on a keyboard that actually feels solid and comfortable — and I’m making a lot less errors than I usually do. But I also wish I had waited a year to upgrade, because the difference between last year’s MacBook Pro that I use for work and this new model is night and day.
